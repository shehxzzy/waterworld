FOUNDATION

Sanjalika Waterpark has been causing quite a noisy stir ever since its foundation was laid due to the thrill of the rides and being one of the first popular water parks of this country. Its foundation is dated back till 1990 and so it has been operating for nearly 30 years!



SAFETY COMES FIRST

We take safety very seriously. All our rides are checked and certified for electrical, mechanical and structural safety. We also hires an outside consultant to check safety and integrity of all slides and dry rides every year.
