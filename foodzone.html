Multicuisine Restaurant, Fast food, and Beverages, Gaming zone


The food zone at Sanjalika Waterpark is spread in a total built-up area of 18800 sq. ft. There are 9 food stalls include a wide wash yard and gas bank area for national and multinational brands – a very hygienic and latest kitchen systems. The Dining area is centrally AC with a dining facility for approx. 300 persons. Also, there are 6 food stalls outside with carpet area of 145 sq. ft. each. The parking area is having a capacity of parking for 350+ cars.
